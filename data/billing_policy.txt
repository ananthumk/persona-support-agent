BILLING POLICY

1. SUBSCRIPTION PLANS

Free Plan
- Cost: $0/month
- Features: Up to 100 API calls/month, Basic support
- Storage: 1 GB
- Limitations: No data export, basic analytics

Standard Plan
- Cost: $29/month (billed monthly) or $290/year (billed annually, saves 17%)
- Features: 10,000 API calls/month, Priority email support
- Storage: 100 GB
- Includes: Advanced analytics, data export
- Auto-renewal: Yes, cancel anytime before next billing cycle

Professional Plan
- Cost: $99/month (billed monthly) or $990/year (billed annually, saves 17%)
- Features: 100,000 API calls/month, Phone support (9am-6pm EST)
- Storage: 1 TB
- Includes: Custom integrations, SSO, IP whitelisting
- Auto-renewal: Yes, dedicated account manager included

Enterprise Plan
- Cost: Custom pricing based on requirements
- Features: Unlimited API calls, 24/7 dedicated support
- Storage: Unlimited
- Includes: Custom SLA, on-premise deployment option, compliance certifications
- Negotiated contract terms apply

2. BILLING CYCLE & PAYMENTS

- Billing occurs on the 1st of each month
- Payment methods accepted: Credit card (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Bank transfer (Enterprise)
- Invoices sent 5 days before payment due date
- Failed payment: Account suspended after 48 hours, re-enabled within 24 hours of successful payment
- Billing timezone: UTC

3. REFUND POLICY

- Full refund available within 7 days of purchase, no questions asked
- Mid-cycle downgrade: Pro-rated refund issued for unused portion
- No refund for service suspension due to ToS violations
- Refunds processed within 5-10 business days to original payment method

4. USAGE OVERAGE

- Standard plan overage: $0.05 per additional 100 API calls
- Professional plan overage: $0.03 per additional 100 API calls
- Enterprise: Included in contract terms
- Customers notified when reaching 80% of monthly quota
- Automatic overage billing unless usage limits configured

5. CANCELLATION & TERMINATION

- Cancel anytime from account settings
- No early termination fees
- Data access remains available for 30 days after cancellation
- Full data export available before account deletion
- All recurring charges cease immediately upon cancellation

6. TAXES & FEES

- Prices shown exclude applicable sales tax/VAT
- Tax rates calculated at checkout based on billing address
- Invoices include tax details and itemization
- US: Sales tax collected in applicable states
- EU: VAT applied per MOSS regulations
- Non-profit and educational institutions: Tax exemption available (requires documentation)

7. PRICE CHANGES

- 30-day notice provided for price increases
- Existing customers locked at current rate for 12 months
- New pricing applies to renewal after 12-month period
- Customers may cancel within 30 days at no penalty if price increases >10%

8. PAYMENT ISSUES & SUPPORT

- Billing inquiries: billing@example.com
- Response time: 24 hours for billing questions
- Payment method update available in account dashboard
- Disputes must be reported within 30 days of charge
