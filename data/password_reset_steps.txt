PASSWORD RESET PROCEDURES

==== WEB PORTAL ====

Step 1: Navigate to Login Page
- Go to https://app.example.com/login
- Click "Forgot Password?" link below login fields

Step 2: Enter Email Address
- Enter the email address associated with your account
- Click "Send Reset Link"
- Confirmation message: "Check your email for reset instructions"

Step 3: Check Email
- Look for email from noreply@example.com
- Subject line: "Password Reset Request - Action Required"
- Email arrives within 5 minutes (check spam folder if not received)
- Reset link expires after 30 minutes

Step 4: Click Reset Link
- Open the email and click the reset link
- Link format: https://app.example.com/reset?token=xxx
- Must click within 30 minutes or link will expire

Step 5: Create New Password
- Password requirements:
  * Minimum 12 characters
  * At least 1 uppercase letter (A-Z)
  * At least 1 lowercase letter (a-z)
  * At least 1 number (0-9)
  * At least 1 special character (!@#$%^&*)
  * Cannot contain username or email
  * Cannot be same as previous 5 passwords
- Confirm password by typing twice
- Click "Reset Password"

Step 6: Success
- Message: "Password successfully changed"
- Automatically redirected to login page
- Log in with new password

==== MOBILE APP ====

1. Open app and tap "Login"
2. Tap "Can't access account?"
3. Enter email address
4. Tap "Send Reset Email"
5. Check email for reset link (expires in 30 minutes)
6. Tap link in email (opens app automatically)
7. Enter new password (same requirements as web)
8. Tap "Confirm"
9. Automatically logged in with new password

==== ALTERNATIVE: SETTINGS MENU ====

1. Log in to account
2. Go to Settings > Security > Password
3. Click "Change Password"
4. Enter current password for verification
5. Enter new password twice
6. Click "Update Password"
7. Session will be logged out on all devices
8. Log back in with new password

==== TROUBLESHOOTING ====

Reset Link Not Received:
- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify email address is correct
- Wait up to 5 minutes
- Try resending link (can request unlimited times)
- Whitelist noreply@example.com in email filters

Reset Link Expired:
- Request new reset link from login page
- Previous link automatically invalidated
- New link sent within 5 minutes

Password Doesn't Meet Requirements:
- Review requirements shown on password field
- Common issues: special character missing, too short, contains username
- Re-enter password meeting all criteria

Still Can't Reset Password:
- Contact support: support@example.com or live chat
- Provide email address and account ID if available
- Support response time: Within 4 business hours
- Admin can manually reset (requires identity verification)

==== SECURITY NOTES ====

- Never share reset links with anyone
- Reset links are unique, one-time use
- Changing password logs out all active sessions
- Mobile and web sessions sync within 30 seconds
- For accounts with 2FA enabled, you'll receive SMS code after password reset
- Failed login attempts after 5 times: Account temporarily locked for 15 minutes
